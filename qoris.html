Qoris® ERM

Qoris® ERM is Udbhata's highly differentiated cloud based SaaS offering. Every feature in Qoris® ERM has been designed by risk practitioners and industry/domain experts. Qoris® ERM is the only software that combines governance, intelligence and integration with decision making in one seamless architecture. With Qoris® ERM experience a powerful way to deal with enterprise risks and decisions.

Qoris® ERM Comply instantly gets your Organisaton started on a very productive ERM journey. Whether you're new to ERM or have implemented several cycles, you immediately benefit from the power of automation and from making your ERM process objective and person independent


Use Qoris® ERM Comply to

  • Translate valuable organisation FTE that is used in preparation and analysing risk information
  • Instantly introduce transparency and repeatability into your Organiations ERM process.
  • Prioritise your Organisation's risk management efforts and build accountability for risk
  • Review your Organisation's current risk information 24/7.

Qoris® ERM Comply+ introduces Organisations to the power of Risk quantification. These are risks that immediately matter to your Organisation.


Use Qoris® ERM Comply+ to

  • Quantify risks, assign risk appetite and tolerance levels at a business, geography or aggregated levels across the Organisaiton
  • Dynamically measure your Organisation's risk exposure in $ ( or your Org currency).
  • Acess all the capabilities of Qoris® ERM Comply

Qoris® ERM Differentiate empowers your Organisation's business and commercial teams to understand and benefit from peers experiences on risk within seconds.


Use Qoris® ERM Differentiate to

  • Validate your risk prioritisation or mitigation approaches by getting to know from the public domain what peers say about their risks.
  • Build in the power of benchmarking risk information
  • Acess all the capabilities of Qoris® ERM Comply+
