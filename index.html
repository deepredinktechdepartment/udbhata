About us

Udbhata is a risk technologies and consulting company based in India. Operating in the confluence of risk management practice, technology, and risk information, our vision is to be a high-quality risk technologies player. We research, develop and commercialize solutions that provide a timely and meaningful benefit to businesses or society. Our primary choice of solutions is driven by a beneficial Total Cost of Ownership for our customers and clients.

Anand, Vijay, and Sumana (current leadership team) collectively bring in over six decades of their in the risk management and technology domains and are invested in researching, determining, and designing meaningful solutions to help Udbhata’s customers and clients succeed.
